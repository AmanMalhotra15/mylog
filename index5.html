Karnal Haveli

Famous food of Karnal Haveli

KARNAL HAVELI is a unique Punjabi theme resort, designed with intricate details to capture the authentic essence of Punjabi culture. It was built with the vision to keep the rich traditions and culture of Punjab alive for generations to come.

It bestows one of largest Punjabi Theme Dhabha with over 300 covers which serves purely vegetarian food and only non-alcoholic beverages. Traditional Chefs specialising in Indian food prepare exotic dishes with a rare ingredient : AFFECTION.



       

Haveli's fast food is also a gourmets delight. One can enjoy the different variety of food like continental, chinese, south indian, tandoori, indian snacks, cool shakes & moctail etc.
